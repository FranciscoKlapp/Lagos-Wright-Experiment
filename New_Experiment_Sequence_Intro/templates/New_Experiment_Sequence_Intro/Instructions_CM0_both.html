Token Exchange — Overview
• All {{session.num_participants}} participants have the opportunity to interact in a single Centralized Token Exchange

• Each participant decides in each round whether to become a:
        - Token Buyer: Use some of your Points to bid for Tokens
        - Token Seller: Sell some of your Tokens to get Points

How many Tokens you get as Token Buyer or how many Points as a Token Seller depends on the resulting Market Price (Tokens per Point)

• Market Price depends on current demand and supply and is only known to you after you make your decision

REMEMBER: You DO NOT KNOW what the Market Price will be when you are deciding how much to bid or sell. It is only determined afterwards, so you'd want to think about what it will be.
