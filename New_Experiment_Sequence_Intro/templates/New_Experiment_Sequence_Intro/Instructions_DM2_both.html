Goods Market — Trading the Special Good
First, each Consumer makes a "take-it-or-leave-it" offer to their counterpart requesting some amount of {{Constants.DM_good}} in exchange for Tokens
• Consumers can get Points from buying the {{Constants.DM_good}} but give up the offered Tokens

• Producers would incur a cost of 1 Point per unit of {{Constants.DM_good}} produced but receive Tokens


Then, each Producer must decide whether to “Accept” or “Reject” their counterpart's proposal:
• If a Producer "Accepts", the proposed exchange takes place

• If a Producer "Rejects" , then no trade takes place (no change in Points or Tokens holdings).
