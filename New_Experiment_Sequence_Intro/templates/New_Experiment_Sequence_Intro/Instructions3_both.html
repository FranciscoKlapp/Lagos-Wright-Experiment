General Overview III - Round Structure (we are getting there...)
• Each round will consist of two parts:

In the first part of each round:
The {{session.num_participants}} participants will be randomly and anonymously matched in {{pairs}} pairs and interact 1-on-1 in Decentralized Goods Market to trade the Special Good

--> This is where NEW additional Points (Surpluses) are created by trading!
In the second and final part of each round:
All {{session.num_participants}} participants will interact together in a Centralized Token Exchange and trade Points for Tokens directly



--> This is where you can adjust your Token holdings for the next round but where NO NEW additional Points are created!
